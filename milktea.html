Our Brand

The brand story of Koi Thé

KOI's mission is probably to spread happiness to everyone. Every special moment, every day that comes to us is an opportunity to experience and learn. And it would be great if your happy time had the companion and appearance of KOI.

With natural ingredients, the selection in each tea leaf has helped KOI bring the most characteristic flavors, helping you to have the most comfortable spirit to be able to experience every moment of your life. That's what KOI wants and Khloe Ma wants to do.

Not only that, the happiness of listening to soft music plus the sound of the collision of ice cubes in the cup, the aroma of tea, the sound of laughter coming from the shop. Those are all the sounds that KOI wants to hear and share with everyone. Each of those moments is captured, encapsulated by KOI in each of his drinks and shared by KOI with everyone. And those simple happy things are only available in KOI.
